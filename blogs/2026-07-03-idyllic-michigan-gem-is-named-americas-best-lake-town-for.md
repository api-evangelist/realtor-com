This Independence Day marks the 250th birthday of the United States , but in Traverse City, MI, the date has special local significance as well. This year is the city's 100th annual Cherry Festival, a weeklong celebration of local cherry farmers , which kicks off on July 4th . There'll be an air show, parades, races, and concerts, with KC and the Sunshine Band , David Lee Roth , Bow Wow , and Soulja Boy slated to perform.
